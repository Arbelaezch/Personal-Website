Triumph of the Will (1934)


The infamous propaganda film of the 1934 Nazi Party rally in Nuremberg, Germany.

Director: Leni Riefenstahl

Writers: Leni Riefenstahl, Walter Ruttmann

Stars: Adolf Hitler, Hermann Göring, Max Amann

Country: Germany

Studio: Universum Film AG (UFA)

The Nazi propaganda film about Hitler reaching 700,000 of his followers in Nuremberg a couple of September days in 1934. With little dialogue, Hitler's Nazi Germany appeals to Germans through a picture of strength and unity. In the midst of a disenfranchised Germany, Hiter and Riefenstahl knew what was necessary to pursuade viewers was an image of a strong, mass movement. Together they could be great again, he promised.

Its a powerful film, and thats whats so scary.
