Quiz Show (1994)

7


A young lawyer, Richard Goodwin, investigates a potentially fixed game show. Charles Van Doren, a big time show winner, is under Goodwin's investigation.

Director: Robert Redford

Writers: Paul Attanasio, Richard N. Goodwin

Stars: Ralph Fiennes, John Turturro, Rob Morrow

Country: America

Produced by: Robert Redford

This is a good film. It's well made and entertaining throughout, and yet I feel less than sold on it. Quiz Show is both an investigative film as well as a study on greed and show-business. It gives a good amount of time to both ventures, though maybe leans a little more heavily on the show-business side.

Of course corruption in showbusiness is by default an interesting topic. You get both glitz and high stakes, as well as a classic downfall tale of one of everybody's favorite pass-time. Television. Director Robert Redford's take on this topic is an interesting one.
