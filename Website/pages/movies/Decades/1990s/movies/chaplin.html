Chaplin (1992)


A film about the troubled and controversial life of the master comedy filmmaker Charles Chaplin.

Director: Richard Attenborough

Writers: David Robinson

Stars: Robert Downey Jr., Geraldine Chaplin, Paul Rhys

Country: America

Producer: Richard Attenborough

Studio: Tristar Pictures



-Charming but a little over the top one the "feels" side of things. -Super by the numbers. You already know exactly how the film is gonna go before you start. -Idk whats up with the audio but everything sounds off. The copy I'm watching makes it sound like ever single line of dialogue was added in in post, rather than naturally performed by the actor. -Whats up with the wipes??? -Downey really is wonderful, however. -Its a well made movie but its also boring. There is nothing special about it other than the fact its about Chaplin. Its safe as safe can be, with the best moments coming from the short performances that Chaplin does to entertain the audience within the film.
