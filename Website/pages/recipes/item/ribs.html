Pork Back Ribs

Here they are, the best ribs this side of the Mississippi. The cream of the the crop. The inspiration for this entire website as well as my love for food. Every bit worth the preparation.

Serves four people.

Ingredients

  • Pork baby back ribs from Safeway
  • Sweet Baby Ray's BBQ Sauce

Rub Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp Chili Powder
  • 1 Tbsp Paprika
  • 1 Tbsp Cumin

Method

Instant Pot Method

  1. Peel off rib membrane
  2. Rub the rub into the ribs, applying a little more to the meatier side.
  3. Roll ribs and put them on their side in the Instant Pot with 2 cups of water.
  4. Close lid and set machine for 18min. Wait 15 minutes after done cooking, then take off lid.
  5. Put ribs on cookie sheet and liberally apply BBQ sauce.
  6. Put into oven at 375F for 30 minutes.
  7. Take out, let it sit for 5 minutes, and you're all good to Enjoy! :)

Oven Method

    1. Take membrane off.
    2. Apply rub.
    3. Wrap ribs in tin foil, ensure tight wrap. Curl in the sides for no leaking.
    4. Put in oven at 275F for 4-4.5 hours.
    5. Take out and let sit for 30 minutes.
    6. Drain tin foil into the sink, and place both racks on one tin foil covered baking sheet.
    7. Liberally brush over both sides with BBQ sauce.
    8. Put back into the oven bone side down, meat side up for 30 minutes at 350F.
