Chicken Quesadilla

Is it chicken? Is it rice? Nay, tis a delicious amalgamation of both that delivers not just a satisfying experience, but an addicting one.

Serves four people.

Ingredients

  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 4 tbsp sesame oil
  • 4 tbsp soya sauce
  • 1 white onion
  • 4.5 cups cooked rice (just less than 2 cups uncooked)
  • 3 chicken breasts (shredded)
  • 4 eggs
  • 2-3 cups frozen veg (carrots, peas)

(Optional)

  • Green Onion
  • Sesame seeds

Method

Rice

  1. Rinse two cups of rice
  2. Coat the bottom of the rice cooker with oil.
  3. Add half of a chopped small white onion.
  4. Add 5 cups of water.
  5. Cook rice and let sit for 10 minutes after done.
  6. Put rice on baking sheet and leave in fridge for 4 hours.

Putting it Together

  1. Cook chicken at 375F for 35 minutes.
  2. Lightly beat four eggs and scramble in an oiled pan until just barely curdled. Don't overcook. Set aside in separate bowl.
  3. Add 3 tbsp of sesame oil to an heated, oiled pan
  4. Add 2-3 cups of frozen vegetables and half of a small chopped white onion.
  5. Sautee for 3-5 minutes on med-high heat.
  6. Add 3 cloves of crushed garlic and fry for 30 seconds
  7. Add rice and 4 tbsp of soya sauce. Stir in well and break up any big clumps.
  8. Add in egg and shredded chicken and mix well.
  9. If dry add more sesame oil and/or soya sauce as needed.
  10. OPTIONAL: Add green onion and sesame seeds and Enjoy :)
